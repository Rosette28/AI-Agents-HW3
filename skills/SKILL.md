## Academic Formatting Guidelines

1. **LaTeX Math:** Ensure all complex equations are wrapped in `\begin{equation}` and `\end{equation}`. Do not use plain text for math.
2. **TikZ Block Diagrams:** You must verify that any block diagrams generated by the Writer use valid `\begin{tikzpicture}` syntax.
3. **BiDi (Hebrew/English) Integrity:** When reviewing the required bilingual chapters, ensure Hebrew text is structurally sound and technical English terms remain Left-to-Right.
4. **Table Limits:** Ensure markdown tables do not exceed standard page widths.
